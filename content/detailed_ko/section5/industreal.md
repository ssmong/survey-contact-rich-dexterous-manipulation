### 5.10 IndustReal

**전체 제목:** IndustReal: Transferring Contact-Rich Assembly Tasks from Simulation to Reality

**저자:** Bingjie Tang, Michael A. Lin, Iretiayo Akinola, Ankur Handa, Gaurav S. Sukhatme, Fabio Ramos, Dieter Fox, Yashraj Narang (NVIDIA NVLabs)

**학회/연도:** RSS 2023

**K/D 결정 방법:** Fixed (hand-tuned). IndustReal uses a fixed impedance controller as the low-level executor. The RL agent (PPO) learns desired poses that are tracked by the impedance controller with constant K and D. The impedance parameters are tuned by hand for the specific assembly tasks. The key contribution is not impedance learning but sim-to-real transfer for contact-rich assembly with fixed impedance.

**출력:** Desired end-effector poses (position targets) generated by the RL policy. Stiffness K and damping D are fixed constants in the impedance controller, not outputs of the learned policy.

**로봇 플랫폼:** Franka Emika Panda + parallel-jaw gripper. No dexterous hand.

**작업:** Industrial assembly tasks: NIST peg insertion (round, square, triangular), gear meshing, PCB connector insertion. These are standardized contact-rich assembly benchmarks.

**핵심 방법론:** IndustReal addresses sim-to-real transfer for contact-rich assembly by combining three techniques: (1) SDF-based contact simulation in IsaacGym for realistic contact modeling, (2) compliance-aware policy training where the RL agent is trained with the same impedance controller that will be used in deployment, and (3) automated real-world evaluation using a standardized NIST assembly board. The fixed impedance controller provides a consistent interface between simulation and reality, reducing the sim-to-real gap for contact-rich tasks. The RL agent learns to exploit the impedance controller's compliance for insertion (e.g., spiral search patterns enabled by lateral compliance).

**아키텍처/파라미터:** PPO-trained policy in IsaacGym with GPU-parallelized environments. Policy MLP: ~256-256. Action space: 6D desired EE pose. Fixed Cartesian impedance controller (hand-tuned K, D). SDF-based contact simulation. Real-robot evaluation on Franka Panda with NIST assembly board.

**주요 기여:**
- Demonstrates successful sim-to-real transfer for contact-rich industrial assembly using RL + fixed impedance control
- SDF-based contact simulation in IsaacGym improves contact modeling fidelity for assembly tasks
- Automated real-world evaluation framework using standardized NIST assembly benchmarks
- Open-source code and trained RL policies (github.com/NVlabs/industreallib)

**한계점:**
- Fixed K and D (hand-tuned) -- impedance is not learned or adapted
- No dexterous hand -- Franka + gripper
- Task-specific impedance tuning required for each assembly type
- RL policies are narrow: trained per task, no cross-task generalization
- Does not demonstrate variable impedance; compliance is useful but static

**결과:** IndustReal achieved high success rates on sim-to-real transfer for NIST assembly tasks: >90% on round peg insertion, >85% on gear meshing, with zero real-world training data. The fixed impedance controller was essential for successful sim-to-real transfer -- position-controlled baselines failed due to contact sensitivity. Code and RL policies at github.com/NVlabs/industreallib.

## 추론 / 배포

- **추론 지연 시간:** Not explicitly reported. The RL policy is a standard MLP (~256-256), running in <1ms per forward pass. Training uses GPU-parallelized IsaacGym environments.
- **배포 하드웨어:** Franka Emika Panda for real-robot evaluation. Training in IsaacGym on NVIDIA GPUs. The MLP policy is lightweight enough for deployment on any compute platform.
- **실시간 가능 여부:** Yes. The MLP policy generates desired EE poses at high frequency, and the fixed Cartesian impedance controller runs at the Franka's native 1 kHz control rate. Successful real-robot deployment demonstrated on NIST assembly tasks.

## 데이터셋 / 데이터 수집

- **사용 데이터셋:** No pre-collected dataset. Pure RL in simulation -- all training data generated via GPU-parallelized IsaacGym simulation with SDF-based contact modeling. Zero real-world training data.
- **수집 방법:** Pure RL (PPO) in IsaacGym with SDF-based contact simulation. No human demonstrations. Sim-to-real transfer evaluated on real Franka Panda with NIST assembly board. The compliance-aware policy training uses the same impedance controller in simulation as in deployment.
- **데이터 규모:** Massively parallel RL training in IsaacGym. Specific episode counts not reported. Evaluation on standardized NIST assembly benchmarks (round, square, triangular pegs; gear meshing; PCB connector insertion).
- **원격 조작 장비:** Not applicable (pure RL, no demonstrations).
- **데이터 포맷:** Not applicable (online RL, no offline dataset).
- **공개 여부:** Yes -- code and trained RL policies at https://github.com/NVlabs/industreallib
